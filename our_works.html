OUR WORKS

WE DO A LOT OF WORKS FOR THE WELL BEING OF CHILDREN AMONG WHICH A FEW ARE LISTED HERE

HEALTHCARE

We provide crucial healthcare support to children, including newborns, infants, young children and adolescents. We cover the medical expenses of abandoned, homeless and destitute children.

EDUCATION

We support the education of the most marginalized children of Nigeria, ensuring that they go to school, stay there and learn to their full potential

REHABILITATION

During emergencies, when children are the most vulnerable, we provide life-saving relief and rehabilitation support to them and their communities

AWARENESS

We counsel parents coming from the Economically Weaker Sections of the society on the significance of sending their children to school and assist them through the admission process of the most marginalized children of Nigeria, ensuring that they go to school and learn

DISASTER MITIGATION

During emergencies, we set up Temporary Learning Centres and distribute education kits so that that the affected children do not drift away from the path of education.

FAMILIARISATION

We are creating an awareness on helping children and families cope with sweeping school closures by providing parents with guidance and tools to reassure and support their children during this difficult time.

ORPHANAGES

In every year we used to visit orphanages situated in different parts of Abuja during festivals and provide the children new clothes and one time meal. We also works with children in orphanages and shelter homes across Abuja and provides them education to fulfills the ambitions of poor child.

BENEFICIARY PROTECTION

We support beneficiaries including women, children and LGBT communities, who have been affected by HIV & AIDS, trafficking, sexual violence, and poverty by providing well sufficient nutrition and give a dignified life to HIV infected children in Nigeria.
