ABOUT OUR ORGANISATION

SALIUKATE FOUNDATION

Saliukate Foundation is a nonprofit charity organisation established to raise funds to enrich the lives of children, youths,women in the areas of health, arts, sports and education. The Foundation, through its grantmaking and volunteerism programs, provides grants, expertise, resources and tools, enabling these nonprofits to not only raise their profile but also further their mission and objectives.

There are numerous Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) in Nigeria working on the issues of children. Out of them, Saliukate Foundation has carved a niche for itself and has emerged as the leading independent child rights organization, clocking presence in the capital of Nigeria (FCT) Abuja.Saliukate foundation works with the vision to build a world in which every child attains the right to survival, protection, development and participation. As the most trusted NGO for children,Saliukate Foundation goes right into the middle of the most backward communities of Nigeria and works to improve the lives of the most disadvantaged children by providing them opportunities to learn, get good healthcare, remain away from harm and giving them much-needed humanitarian relief during disasters.
